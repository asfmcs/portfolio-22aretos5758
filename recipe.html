Shrimp

Ingredients Steps Image Source
  • 20 jumbo shrimp peeled
  • 4 roma tomatoes finely chopped
  • 2-3 fresh garlic cloves minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp fresh chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup water optional
  • Apply oil and garlic to the pan. Stir.
  • Apply tomato into pan. Stir.
  • Apply Shrimp and Vinegar. Stir.
  • Leave cooking for 2-3 minutes.
  • Apply Salt and Pepper. Stir.
  • Gently apply Parsley

    Salmon

    Ingredients Steps Image Source
    • Salmon Fillet
    • Salt and Pepper
    • 1 Table Spoon of Butter
  • Season Salmon Fillet with Salt and Pepper.
  • Apply Butter to pan.
  • Put Salmon Fillet into Pan.
  • Leave cooking for about 6 minutes.
  • Flip.
  • Leave cooking for 2-4 minutes.

    Ceviche

    Ingredients Steps Image Source
    • 1 Pound Cooked Medium Shrimp
    • 1 Chopped Avocado
    • 1 Medium Chopped Tomato
    • 1 Serreno Chile Sliced
    • 1/2 Chopped English Cucumbers
    • 1/2 Cup of Red Onion
    • 1/4 Cup of Cilantro
    • 1/2 Teaspoon of Dried Oregano
    • 8 Tostada Shells
    • 1/2 Crumbled Feta Cheese
  • Mix everything in medium bowl, except Feta Cheese and Shrimp.
  • Let it marinate for 10 minutes.
  • Gently apply Cheese in Tostada shell.
  • Apply Shrimp into middle.
  • Apply mixture around shrimp and organize.
